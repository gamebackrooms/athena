Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/13/2024

Terminal of Games ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and share that information, and the choices you have regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide an optimal experience and prevent abuse on the Platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • IP Address: We collect your IP address when you access the Platform. This information helps us improve user experience, detect and prevent fraudulent activities, and protect the integrity of our community.
  • Device and Usage Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the Platform, including browser type, operating system, and usage data. This information helps us optimize our Platform for different devices and provide a smoother user experience.
  • Token and Account Information: If you interact with the Platform using GAME Tokens, we may collect token balance and transaction data to enable certain features and verify your access rights.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Enhance User Experience: By understanding how users interact with our Platform, we can make improvements and optimize the experience for all users.
  • To Prevent Abuse and Fraud: Collecting IP addresses and usage data allows us to monitor for unusual or unauthorized activity, helping us keep the Platform secure and prevent abusive behavior.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use and disclose your information as required by applicable laws and regulations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Platform, such as hosting providers and analytics services. These service providers are required to protect your information and use it only as directed by us.
  • Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to legal proceedings. Additionally, we may disclose information to enforce our Terms of Service, prevent fraud, or protect the safety of our users.

4. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet-based service is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You may limit certain types of data collection by adjusting your browser settings to prevent tracking. Please note that this may affect your ability to use some features of the Platform.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our data practices.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at gm_terminal.

By using Terminal of Games, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
